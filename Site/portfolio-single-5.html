OFFICIAL FINDERS

  • WordPress implementation (based on the Genesis theme)
  • Responsive design using Bootstrap
  • Integration of social media feed

"Nice and simple. I hate busy sites!"- Client

This site provided a challenge, but one that I was ecstatic to accept! The clients wanted a website that incorporates a lot of information but is also clean and easy to navigate. The site needed to accommodate a few different types of users, including officials and players.

The finished product (well, what is "finished"?) allows the clients to update rules for different sports and to update the Calendar page with events. Information is easily accessible without having to click through numerous pages. It was a blast to work with these clients and on this site, and in the end, it was bittersweet to hand it off to them!
